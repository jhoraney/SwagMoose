Amusement Parks and Museums in Iowa

There are tons of fun amusement parks and Museums for you to go and hang out!

So you need a place to have a ton of fun. We can find some places for you to have the best time possible. There are places like Adventureland or Lost Island. But, maybe you prefer museums so we have a few options like Amana Colonies, the Mississippi River Museum and Aquarium or University Iowa Museum. So you definitely have more than a few places to choose from whether you would like to go to Amusement park or Museum.

List of Amusement Parks

Adventureland
- A fun filled amusement park with a water park inside. Together the two contain over 100 rides that will bring joy among everyone who visits the park.
Lost Island
- A waterpark with many slides,wave pools, ziplining, and go-karts! Allowing you to have tons of fun in the sun!
- Waterpark: 10:30 a.m. – 6:30 p.m.
- Golf, Go Karts & Ziplining: 2 p.m. – 9 p.m.
~Friday: 6 p.m. – 9 p.m.
~Saturday: 2 p.m. – 9 p.m.
~Sunday: 2 p.m. – 7 p.m.
Planet X Family Fun Center
- A fun area to play laser tag and other fun games.
Pzazz FunCity
FunCity features many attractions for the whole family to enjoy. The kids will be sure to love over 100 interactive arcade games, kiddie go-carts and multi-level laser tag arena. FunCity's truly got it all!


List of Museums

Mississippi River Museums and Aquarium
The museum is a place where you can go by the Mississippi River and learn about it
Amana Colonies
Today, Amana is a major tourist attraction known mainly for its restaurants and craft shops. Included in the shops are woodworking shops, wine shops, and even a brewery called Millstream. The colonies as a whole have been listed as a National Historic Landmark since 1965.
Iowa Aviation Museum
This museum is all about early planes.
Monday-Saturday 10 a.m to 5 p.m.
