There are tons of fun amusement parks and Museums for you to go and hang out!

List of Amusement Parks

Adventureland

Amusementpark + Water Park
3200 Adventureland Dr, Altoona, IA 50009
A fun filled amusement park with a water park inside. Together the two contain over 100 rides that will bring joy among everyone who visits the park.

Lost Island

2225 E Shaulis Rd, Waterloo, IA 50701
A waterpark with many slides,wave pools, ziplining, and go-karts! Allowing you to have tons of fun in the sun!
Hours
Waterpark: 10:30 a.m. – 6:30 p.m.
Golf, Go Karts & Ziplining: 2 p.m. – 9 p.m.
Friday: 6 p.m. – 9 p.m.
Saturday: 2 p.m. – 9 p.m.
Sunday: 2 p.m. – 7 p.m.

Planet X Family Fun Center

4444 1st Ave NE Suite 3001 Cedar Rapids, IA 52402
A fun area to play laser tag and other fun games.
Monday - Thursday: 11am - 10pm
Friday: 11am - Midnight
Saturday: 10am - Midnight
Sunday: 11am - 9pm

Pzazz FunCity

3001 Winegard Drive Burlington, IA 52601
FunCity features many attractions for the whole family to enjoy. The kids will be sure to love over 100 interactive arcade games, kiddie go-carts and multi-level laser tag arena. FunCity's truly got it all!
