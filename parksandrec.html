List of Amusement Parks

Adventureland

Amusementpark + Water Park
3200 Adventureland Dr, Altoona, IA 50009
A fun filled amusement park with a water park inside. Together the two contain over 100 rides that will bring joy among everyone who visits the park.

Pzazz FunCity
FunCity features many attractions for the whole family to enjoy. The kids will be sure to love over 100 interactive arcade games, kiddie go-carts and multi-level laser tag arena. FunCity's truly got it all!


List of Museums

Mississippi River Museums and Aquarium
The museum is a place where you can go by the Mississippi River and learn about it
Amana Colonies
Today, Amana is a major tourist attraction known mainly for its restaurants and craft shops. Included in the shops are woodworking shops, wine shops, and even a brewery called Millstream. The colonies as a whole have been listed as a National Historic Landmark since 1965.
Iowa Aviation Museum
This museum is all about early planes.
